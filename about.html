About Simple Retirement Calculators

Retirement planning boils down to a handful of questions: How much do I need? When will I have it? Will it last? Simple Retirement Calculators answers each one with a fast, free tool — no accounts, no signups, no spreadsheets.

Everything runs instantly in your browser. Nothing you type is sent anywhere or stored. Enter your numbers, get your answer, and share a link to your results if you'd like.

The tools:

  • Retirement Number — your target nest egg, based on the 4% rule with an adjustable withdrawal rate.
  • When Can I Retire? — the year your savings and contributions reach your target.
  • Compound Interest — how a balance plus regular contributions grows over time.
  • Drawdown — how long your nest egg lasts once you start withdrawing.
  • Coast FIRE — whether your current savings could grow to your goal with no further contributions.
  • The 4% Rule — where the famous guideline comes from and how to apply it.
